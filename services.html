Service FAQs

Quick answers about our postal services

Speed Post is a priority express service guaranteeing delivery within 1–4 business days with full tracking. Registered Post is slower but provides legal proof of posting and requires the recipient's acknowledgement signature, making it ideal for legal documents.

DIGIPIN is India's 10-digit geocode for precise address identification. During booking, enter your recipient's DIGIPIN alongside their address. This helps our delivery agents locate exact drop points, especially in areas with non-standard addresses.

Yes! Smart India Post supports international parcels and letters to 195+ countries. Pricing varies by destination and weight. All international shipments include customs documentation support and tracking where available.

Yes. All Post Office savings schemes are backed by the Government of India, making them among the safest investment options available. Schemes include PPF, Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana, National Savings Certificate, and more.
